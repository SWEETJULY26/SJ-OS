# About the Business

Sweet July is Ayesha Curry's lifestyle brand. Sweet July Skin, its skincare line, sells Caribbean and Jamaican-rooted skincare ("modern formulas, ancient wisdom") direct-to-consumer via sweetjuly.com and through retail partners including Sephora, Ulta, and Whole Foods.

**Revenue:** Tracked primarily through Shopify (sweetjuly.com) for DTC.

**Target customer:** Women looking for an inclusive, skin-tone-celebrating self-care ritual — warm and "everything irie," not clinical skincare.
