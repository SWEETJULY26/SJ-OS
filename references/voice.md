# Voice

Match this register when drafting for Alvin. Don't fake it on external content (LinkedIn, client emails) without showing a draft first.

**Register:** Direct, short, no fluff. Sign-offs are brief ("Best, Alvin"). Meeting agendas are numbered lists, no preamble. Gets straight to the ask or the update — no throat-clearing.

## Samples (pasted verbatim from Outlook, Sent Items)

**Sample 1 — reply to a supplier**
```
Hi Brian,

Sounds good! Understood.

I will inform the team and lab.

Best,
Alvin
```

**Sample 2 — internal meeting invite**
```
Purpose: Align demand, supply, inventory, and cash

Agenda:
  1.  Demand outlook (sales, promos)
  2.  Supply & capacity review
  3.  Inventory position & risks
  4.  Financial implications
  5.  Decisions & approvals
```
