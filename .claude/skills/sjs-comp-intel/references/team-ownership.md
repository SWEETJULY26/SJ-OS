## If capacity constraints hit

Priority order for what to protect vs what to let slip:

1. **Always protect**: monthly trend digest. This is the stream's heartbeat. Skipping a month signals the stream is dead.
2. **Protect next**: quarterly teardown sign-off moment (the decision point). The teardown can be lighter if needed, but the decision conversation cannot be skipped.
3. **First to slip**: teardown depth on aspirational brands (LANEIGE, YTTP). Direct comps always get full reads; aspirationals can go to lighter format during crunch periods.
4. **Second to slip**: pipeline tracker refresh timing. Can slide by a quarter without signal loss.
5. **Third to slip**: cross-stream monthly check-ins. Can move to async via signal log comments.

## Succession

If Nicole is unavailable for a quarter, Soraya steps up as interim DRI. If both are unavailable, the stream pauses trend digest production for that month and maintains only the signal log. Do not hand the stream to someone who hasn't run it; the analytical judgment is the stream's value and cannot be delegated on short notice.
