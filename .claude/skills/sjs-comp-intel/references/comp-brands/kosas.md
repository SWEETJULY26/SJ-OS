## 1. Identity

Founded 2015 by Sheena Yaitanes. Originally color cosmetics, expanded into skincare roughly 2020-2021. Clean beauty positioning. Available at Sephora and Ulta — the rare prestige brand that is genuinely dual-distribution.

**Positioning**: clean, clinical, hybrid (makeup-skincare crossover).

**Why Kosas is a direct comp**: the only founder led Ulta native brand in the tier. Best signal we have for how Ulta merchants treat brands that look like SJS (founder led, prestige, clean positioning). The category expansion from color into skincare is also a signal for how SJS might approach inverse expansion (skincare into adjacent categories) if we go that direction.
