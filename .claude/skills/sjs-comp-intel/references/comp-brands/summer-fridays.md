## 1. Identity

Founded 2018 by Marianna Hewitt (beauty influencer) and Lauren Gores Ireland. DTC-first launch with the Jet Lag Mask as the single hero SKU. Entered Sephora in 2019. The canonical case study for hero SKU driven brand economics in prestige skincare.

**Positioning**: experiential, editorial, "effortless" skincare. Heavy use of food and travel visual vocabulary.

**Why Summer Fridays is a direct comp**: the benchmark for hero SKU led portfolio economics. Proves that one or two SKUs can carry 60%+ of brand revenue and still support a prestige positioning. The Lip Butter Balm is the current poster child for lip-as-acquisition at a slightly higher price tier ($24) than Rhode. Parallel playbook but different execution.
