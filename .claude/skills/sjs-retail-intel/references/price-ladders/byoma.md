## Pricing pattern analysis

**The Byoma price ladder is the tightest in the cohort — extremely compressed range:**

| Price point | SKU count | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| $13.99 | 2–3 | Cleansers (entry tier) |
| $15.99 | 1 | Toner |
| **$16.99** | **~12 SKUs** | **The core price point — ~30% of the entire assortment** |
| $18.99 | 2 | Eye cream + oil + treatment |
| $19.99 | 1 | SPF (the ceiling SKU) |
| $25.99 | 1 | Ulta-exclusive 3-piece bundle |

**Entire skincare ladder fits in a $13.99–$19.99 window (~$6 range).** This is deliberate — Byoma wants the shopper to pick SKUs by skin concern, not by budget. There is no "premium" or "apex" SKU in the Byoma portfolio. The 3-piece bundle at $25.99 is the only cross-category SKU above $20.

Compare ceilings across the cohort:
- **Byoma $19.99** ← tightest ceiling
- **Naturium $24.99** ← +$5
- **Topicals $38** ← +$18
- **Summer Fridays $82** (fragrance) or $49 (hero mask) ← +$30-62

- **Target Circle Week** — participates in Target's promotional calendar
- **TikTok Shop holiday bundles** — observed in previous holiday seasons

**Read:** The most promo-aggressive brand in the cohort alongside LANEIGE. The contrast with Naturium (same retailer, same category, no standing discount) is the sharpest intra-tier split. Byoma prioritizes velocity over brand discipline; Naturium prioritizes discipline over discounting.

## Cross-channel pricing observations

1. **Price parity across DTC + Target + Ulta + Amazon** on list prices. Sale-pricing differs by retailer.
2. **Ulta is the primary promo channel** — Ulta runs 25% off frequently; Target runs Circle Week mechanics; DTC is the baseline.
3. **Ulta-exclusive SKUs (Foaming Rice Cleanser, Hydrating Trio Set)** are the only channel-specific SKUs. Like Naturium, these are Byoma's retail-partner theater.
4. **No Sephora US column.** This is Byoma's deliberate channel choice — at Sephora UK but not Sephora US. Worth monitoring for change signal.
5. **The sub-$20 ceiling is the structural positioning.** Byoma will not launch a $25-$50 SKU without fundamentally repositioning the brand. This is a moat for SJS if SJS positions at $30+ — Byoma cannot easily follow upmarket without breaking brand.
6. **3-piece Trio bundle $25.99 at $34 value** is the single SKU above $20 in the entire Byoma Ulta assortment. It exists specifically for Ulta bundle-promo slots.
