1. **Price parity is absolute across DTC + Target + Ulta + Amazon.** $16.99 body washes and $24.99 Plant Ceramide Cream are identical on every channel. This is the cleanest price parity in the cohort — no channel gets a "deal" that other channels don't.

3. **The $24.99 price ceiling is strategic, not coincidental.** E.l.f.'s internal pricing discipline explicitly caps Naturium at sub-$25 to preserve the accessible-clean positioning and maximize Ulta shelf-velocity. SJS at $35-$50 sits in a different shelf-dynamic.
4. **No Sephora column.** Naturium is the only major cohort brand built from the ground up to avoid Sephora. This is an E.l.f. bet that Target + Ulta + Amazon + DTC is the full funnel for accessible-clean skincare.
