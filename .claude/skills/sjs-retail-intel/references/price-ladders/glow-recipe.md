## Promo / discount mechanics

- **Welcome offer:** Free Stretch Tote with $55+ first DTC order
- **GWP threshold:** Free Korean Skincare Innovations Kit with $75+ order (12 individual sheet masks)
- **Club Glow Rewards:** loyalty program, points-per-purchase
- **Sephora Savings Events:** participates April + November (10/15/20% by Beauty Insider tier)
- **No standalone DTC sitewide discounts observed** — disciplined posture
- **TikTok Shop:** holiday set discounting observed ($50 holiday set discounted from $70 retail)
- **Active counterfeit defense:** brand explicitly directs consumers to authorized retailers only; flags deep Amazon discounts as red flags

**Read:** Most disciplined promo posture in the cohort alongside Summer Fridays. "Usually $50 or less" is a brand commitment from founders. Promotional muscle goes into bundles + GWP rather than markdowns. Notable contrast with LANEIGE active-promo posture.

## Cross-channel pricing observations

1. **Sephora-DTC-Amazon parity** — $36 Dew Drops and $34 Plum Plump Cream hold across DTC, Sephora, and the official Amazon storefront. Brand actively patrols off-price leakage and counterfeit listings.
2. **Pricing ceiling discipline** — "usually $50 or less" is the explicit founder commitment. Highest verified standard SKU is $36 (Dew Drops). No SKU in the verified ladder breaches $50. This is the tightest pricing discipline in the cohort.
3. **No Ulta column** — Glow Recipe's Sephora-exclusivity is the cleanest in the cohort. Multiple TikTok queries asking "do they sell Glow Recipe at Ulta" + Jan 2026 third-party verification confirm not at Ulta.
4. **Mini hero economics** — $20 mini Dew Drops + $20 mini Plum Plump are the brand's customer-acquisition tier. Per-oz pricing is significantly higher than standard, but the trial-up is intentionally easy.
5. **Refillable Plum Plump Cream is the sustainability + LTV mechanic** — $34 standard refillable jar is the same architecture YTTP uses on its refill pouch and Glow Recipe's only refillable SKU. Likely fast-follows on additional SKUs in the franchise.
6. **New launch pre-pricing risk (4.28.26 Watermelon Peptide Cushion Cream)** — pricing not yet public as of this refresh. Refresh ladder one week post-launch to capture.
