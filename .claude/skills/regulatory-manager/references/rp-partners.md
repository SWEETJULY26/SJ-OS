# International RP partner catalog

Sweet July Skin's international regulatory filings route through country-specific responsible-person (RP) partners. Pedrero Regulatory (Amy Pedrero) acts as the introducer and remains the binding regulatory call for US scope; RP partners own substantive filing work in their countries.

## Engagement model

RP partners are consult-only on the US side. Each partner has its own scope and contract:

- No Asana access, no internal SJS authority, no PLM or Outlook read.
- All engagement flows through Outlook on the Operator's account.
- Pedrero introduces the partner and remains the substantive reviewer on cross-cutting US questions.
- Country-specific filings are owned by the RP partner; Pedrero may consult but does not file outside the US.

## On-call expectation

International RP partners do not carry the same on-call expectation as Pedrero for SAEs and Class I recalls. For agency-side US events affecting Canadian distribution, Pedrero leads and Ecomundo (or alternative) consults on Canadian-side cascade.

## Engagement letter

Each RP partner carries its own engagement letter and annual renewal cadence. First Ecomundo renewal date confirmed at first invocation post-2026-05-12. Renewal lives in Pedrero Liaison section of SJS Regulatory Management (same section, partner identified in title / description until the proposed `RP Partner` field is added).
